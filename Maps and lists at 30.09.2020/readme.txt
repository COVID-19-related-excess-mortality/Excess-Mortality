Maps and lists of excess mortality created from the ISTAT mortality database released on 30.12.2020
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Maps are in .png format; lists are in .xls format.

Filenames are structured as follows:
region_sexageclass_initialdate_finaldate

ITA is used for Italy; F, M, T are used for females, males and total.

As an example, the file "Lom_T80120_01092020_30092020_list.xls" includes excess mortality results for Lombardy, Total 80-120 years, in the period September 1st 2020 to September 30th 2020.
